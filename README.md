<h2 id="ep1">Episode 1 – Farkon Tafiya</h2>

<h3 id="ep1A">Part A – Farkon Tafiya</h3>
<p>
Amina ta tashi da safe, rana na shigowa ta tagar dakinta tana haskaka bangon falo. Hasken na fara bushewa a fuskarta, ya bayyana dariya da tsoro a lokaci guda. Ta tuna maganar mahaifiyarta: <em>"Diyata, gaskiya da jajircewa sune makullin rayuwa."</em>
</p>
<p>
Jakarta a kafadarta, ta yi bankwana da mahaifi da ‘yan uwanta. Kowanne ya danne ta cikin runguma, suna mata addu’a. A kowane mataki, zuciyarta na cike da bege da rashin tabbas. Ta san tafiya zuwa birni babban canji ne a rayuwarta.
</p>
<p>
Da isowarta birni, Amina ta ji kukan mota, mutane masu sauri, kasuwanni da ke cike da hayaniya. Duk da haka, akwai wani yanayi na tsoro da ban mamaki a zuciyarta. Kowanne mataki yana nuna sabuwar rayuwa.
</p>

<h3 id="ep1B">Part B – Hanyoyi Masu Tabbas</h3>
<p>
A makaranta, Amina ta shiga aji mai cike da sababbin fuskoki. Fatima, yarinya mai kyau da kirki, ta yi murmushi ta ce, "Barka da zuwa! Zan taimaka miki idan kina bukata." Suleiman, saurayi mai hankali, ya ba ta shawara da taimako. Amma Hauwa, mai hassada, ta kalli Amina da fuskar rashin yarda.
</p>
<p>
Ranar farko ta wuce cikin koyarwa da shakku. Amina ta fahimci cewa dole ne ta yi hakuri da mutane daban-daban, ta koyi dokokin birni da sabbin abokai. Lokacin da sakamakon karatu na farko ya fito, sunanta ya bayyana a saman allo. Farin ciki ya cika zuciyarta, amma ta lura da wasu murmushi marasa dadi daga Hauwa.
</p>

<h3 id="ep1C">Part C – Sabuwar Fara</h3>
<p>
Daren farko a gidan abokin karatu, Amina ta zauna a ɗaki tana rubutu a littafin diary. Kowanne kalma na bayyana tsoron da bege a lokaci guda. Ta fahimci cewa rayuwa ta fara, kuma kowanne mataki yana dauke da darasi. Ta kalli tagar dakinta, rana ta fara faduwa, hasken dare na kwance a fuskar birni.
</p>
<p>
Ta yi addu’a cikin zuciyarta: "Allah ka kare ni, ka ba ni hikima da ƙarfin zuciya." A wannan dare, Amina ta fara fahimtar cewa kowanne lokaci yana da kalubale, amma gaskiya da jajircewa zasu zama makullin nasara.
</p>

<h2 id="ep2">Episode 2 – Nasara da Nisa</h2>

<h3 id="ep2A">Part A – Sabuwar Rayuwa</h3>
<p>
Bayan hutun summer, Amina ta dawo makaranta. Sabon aji, sababbin fuskoki, sababbin kalubale. Ta dauki numfashi mai zurfi yayin da ta shiga ɗakin karatu. Fatima ta tarbe ta da murmushi mai dumi: "Barka da dawowa! Za mu yi aiki tare a wannan semester." Suleiman kuwa ya tsaya kusa da ita, yana dubanta da ido mai kulawa.
</p>
<p>
Kowanne darasi ya kasance gwaji na hankali da juriya. Amina ta lura da yadda mutane ke kallonta: wasu suna sha’awa, wasu kuma suna hassada. Hauwa ta ci gaba da kallonta da tsantsar rashin yarda, amma Amina ta yi murmushi cikin nutsuwa, ta ce a zuciyarta: <em>"Ba zan bari hassada ta shafi ni ba."</em>
</p>

<h3 id="ep2B">Part B – Jarabawar Nasara</h3>
<p>
Lokacin babban jarabawa ya iso. Amina ta zauna a teburinta, zuciyarta na bugawa da sauri. Mr. Okoye ya rarraba takardun jarabawa, fuskarsa mai tsanani ta sanya kowa cikin shakku.  
</p>
<p>
Hauwa ta yi ƙoƙarin cutar da ita a hankali, ta yi tambayoyi da tsokana, amma Amina ta mayar da hankali, ta gano dukkan matsalolin cikin hikima. Ta rubuta amsoshi da karfin zuciya, tana jin kowane kalma na cika da hikima da natsuwa.
</p>
<p>
Lokacin da aka gama, sakamakon ya fito. Amina ta lashe gasar ilimi! Farin ciki ya cika zuciyarta. Malamai da dalibai sun yaba mata. Ta koyi darasi mai ƙarfi: gaskiya da aiki tukuru sun fi kowanne sirri ko mugunta tasiri.
</p>

<h3 id="ep2C">Part C – Farkon Haske</h3>
<p>
Bayan gasar, Amina ta koma gida tare da Fatima da Suleiman. Mahallinta na cike da farin ciki da murmushi. Mahaifiyarta ta rungume ta da ƙarfi, tana hawaye na farin ciki: "Na san ki za ki iya!"  
</p>
<p>
Amina ta fahimci cewa nasara ba kawai samun kyauta ba ce, amma riƙe gaskiya, aiki tukuru, da haɗin kai. Kowanne ƙaramin aiki na gaskiya yana haifar da babban haske a rayuwa. Daren ya ratsa, birni ya yi shiru, amma zuciyar Amina tana cike da bege da karfin zuciya.
</p>

<h2 id="ep3">Episode 3 – Ci gaba da Sirrin Rayuwa</h2>

<h3 id="ep3A">Part A – Sabuwar Ƙalubale</h3>
<p>
Amina ta shiga sabon ajin da babban ƙalubale. Mr. Okoye ya sanar da babban gasar ilimi mai zuwa, wanda kyautarsa zata kai zuwa tafiya zuwa Jamus. Zuciyarta ta dinga bugawa da sauri – wannan dama ce amma tana da wahala sosai.  
</p>
<p>
Fatima da Suleiman sun tsaya kusa da ita, suna ba da shawarwari da goyon baya: "Ki yi amfani da duk abinda kika koya," Suleiman ya ce. "Mu amince da hikimarki," Fatima ta ƙara.  
</p>
<p>
Amina ta yi murmushi, ta san cewa kowanne mataki yana bukatar jajircewa da hankali. Ta kalli sauran dalibai – wasu suna da kwarewa sosai, wasu suna ƙoƙarin boye damuwarsu. Duk wannan ya sa ta kara himma.
</p>

<h3 id="ep3B">Part B – Ƙarshen Sirri</h3>
<p>
Lokacin gasar ya iso. Amina ta tsai da hankali, ta mayar da hankali kan kowanne tambaya. Hauwa ta yi ƙoƙarin cutar da ita, ta yi tsokana da wasu dabaru, amma Amina ta tsaya cikin natsuwa, ta yi amfani da dabarun da ta koya a baya.  
</p>
<p>
Fatima da Suleiman sun taimaka mata wajen tunawa da abubuwan da suka koya. Kowane mataki na gasar yana bayyana ƙarfin zuciya da basirar Amina. A ƙarshe, ta kammala dukkan aikin ta da kyau sosai.
</p>
<p>
Lokacin da sakamakon ya fito, Amina ta lashe gasar! Farin ciki ya cika zuciyarta, amma mafi muhimmanci, ta gane cewa jajircewa da gaskiya ne suka kawo nasara. Hauwa ta kalli Amina da mamaki, babu wata hanya ta boye cewa Amina ta cancanci nasara.
</p>

<h3 id="ep3C">Part C – Farkon Haske</h3>
<p>
Bayan gasar, Amina ta koma gida, ta rungume mahaifiyarta da ƙarfi. Ta yi hawaye na farin ciki da godiya: "Na yi nasara, amma ba kawai don kyautar tafiya ba, amma don zuciya da aiki tukuru."  
</p>
<p>
Mahaifiyarta ta yi murmushi mai cike da alfahari: "Diyata, na san za ki iya!" A wannan dare, Amina ta fahimci darasi mafi girma: nasara ba kawai samun sakamako ba ce, amma riƙe gaskiya, haɗin kai, da ƙarfin zuciya.  
</p>
<p>
Birni ya yi shiru, dare ya lullube dukkan gida. Amma zuciyar Amina tana cike da bege, karfin zuciya, da sabon haske na rayuwa. Wannan shine farkon sabon babi a rayuwarta – cikakke da darussa, haɗin kai, da bege.
</p>

<h2 id="ep4">Episode 4 – Tafiya zuwa Sabbin Duniya</h2>

<h3 id="ep4A">Part A – Sabuwar Tafiya</h3>
<p>
Amina ta samu labarin wata gasar ƙasa da ƙasa, inda za a aika manyan dalibai zuwa wasu kasashen duniya don karatu da musayar ilimi. Zuciyarta ta dinga bugawa da sauri, yayin da Fatima da Suleiman suka yi murna tare da ita.  
</p>
<p>
"Za mu je Jamus, Faransa, da Ingila," Mr. Okoye ya sanar da ajin. Amina ta ji sabon haske a zuciyarta – wannan dama ce da ba kowa ke samu ba. Ta shirya kayan karatu, littattafai, da jakar da zata dauke ta a wannan tafiya mai ban mamaki.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep4B">Part B – Farkon Tafiya</h3>
<p>
Sun isa filin jirgin sama, birnin da ya cika da sauti da hayaniya. Jiragen suna tashin hankali, mutane suna gudu a ko'ina. Amina ta yi murmushi, tana jin cewa wannan tafiya zata canza rayuwarta.  
</p>
<p>
A cikin jirgin, ta kalli taurari a sama, ta yi addu'a: "Allah ka jagoranci tafiyata, ka ba ni hikima da natsuwa." Ta lura da wasu dalibai daga Japan, Brazil, da Indiya – duk suna da tarihi da labarun daban-daban da zata koya daga gare su.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep4C">Part C – Sabbin Abokai</h3>
<p>
Amina ta fara haɗuwa da abokai daga kasashe daban-daban: Hiroshi daga Japan, Mariana daga Brazil, da Aisha daga Indiya. Kowanne yana da kwarewa da labarun rayuwa daban-daban.  
</p>
<p>
Sun fara tattaunawa game da abubuwan da suka koya a kasashensu, labarun iyali, da al’adun gargajiya. Amina ta ji cewa duniya tana da faɗi sosai, kuma kowanne labari yana koya mata darasi mai mahimmanci.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep4D">Part D – Kalubale a Sabon Ƙasa</h3>
<p>
A lokacin da suka isa Jamus, Amina ta lura da yanayin daban – sanyi, hanyoyi masu tsabta, mutane masu ladabi. Duk da haka, akwai kalubale: harshe, abinci, da sabuwar hanyar karatu.  
</p>
<p>
Amma Fatima da Suleiman suna tare da ita, suna taimaka mata wajen fuskantar kowanne matsala. "Ki yi hakuri da koyo, kowanne sabon abu zai zama dama," Fatima ta ce.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep4E">Part E – Farkon Koyo</h3>
<p>
A makarantar Jamus, Amina ta shiga azuzuwan da suka cika da sabbin dabaru da fasahohi. Malamansu sun yi karatu mai zurfi, suna ƙarfafa su don su fahimci al’adun ƙasa daban-daban.  
</p>
<p>
Ta fara rubuta diary mai cikakken labari, ta rubuta kowane kalmomi da labari, tana jin cewa kowanne rana yana cike da darasi da sabon haske. Ta koyi cewa ilimi ba kawai karatu ba ne, amma haɗin kai da fahimta tsakanin mutane daban-daban.
</p>

<h3 id="ep5A">Part A – Ƙarin Labarai</h3>
<p>

</p>
<p>
Hiroshi daga Japan ya nuna mata dabarun kimiyya, Mariana daga Brazil ta koya mata yadda ake amfani da fasaha wajen tsara labarai, Aisha daga Indiya ta nuna mata dabarun sadarwa da haɗin kai a kungiyoyi.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep5B">Part B – Ƙalubale na Gaskiya</h3>

</p>
<p>
"Mu koyi daga kowanne mutum, kuma mu yi aiki tare," ta ce. Ƙungiyarta ta fara samun nasara, suna kammala aikin da kyau kuma sun sami yabo daga malamai.

<h3 id="ep5C">Part C – Sirrin Gaskiya</h3>

<h3 id="ep6A">Part A – Sabon Ƙalubale</h3>
<p>

</p>
<p>

</p>

<h3 id="ep6B">Part B – Nasarori Masu Dadi</h3>
<p>

</p>
<p>

<p>

</p>
<p>
Amina ta bude wasikar, zuciyarta tana bugawa da sauri. Kalaman mahaifinta sun bayyana gaskiya da dalilin tafiyarsa da nisan shekaru. Ta koyi cewa dukkan abubuwan da suka faru a rayuwarta sun kasance don shiryar da ita zuwa wannan lokaci.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep7B">Part B – Ƙarshen Sirri</h3>
<p>

</p>
<p>
Ta fahimci cewa rayuwa ba kawai samun nasara ba ce, amma fahimtar sirrin gaskiya da darussa daga kowane mataki. Duk wanda ya shiga tafiyarta, ya koyi darasi mai amfani.  
</p>

<h3 id="ep7C">Part C – Farkon Sabon Babi</h3>

</p>

Birni ya yi shiru, dare ya lullube dukkan gida, amma zuciyar Amina tana cike da bege, karfin zuciya, da sabon haske na rayuwa. Wannan shine **farkon sabon babi** a rayuwarta – cikakke da darussa, haɗin kai, da bege.
